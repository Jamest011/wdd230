Target Audience

The target audience is the community and the members within that are looking for businesses.

Whether they are business owners themselves, new to the area or simply just looking up what businesses are in the area.

Scenarios

  1. How close is the nearest UPS, FEDEX or USPS?
  2. Why should I join the local chamber of commerce?
  3. How many of the businesses are members of the commerce?
  4. What resources are there for new business owners?
  5. Are there any local business owners that can mentor?

Personas

Jackie Buckly

Jackie is a 35 year old mother of 5 who runs a small school and is looking to move into a bigger location. She is wanting to learn more about the area and to connect with other businesses in the area to promote other stores to shop at for school supplies and other school related needs. She also wants the new location to be close to other businesses that have shoppers with kids.

Theodore Barker

Theodore is has been in the printing business for more than 25 years. With online shopping on the rise, he is needing to move into a bigger unit that has less of an office and more garage and workshop space. This flex unit would be best away from traffic, but close enough to other businesses that he has been working with over the years. He is seeking out help to see where his suppliers are specifically located and how he can be closer to them while getting a larger space.
