Non-Profit Membership - No Charge:

This membership is for non-profit organizations and there is no fee.

Bronze Membership - $500/year:

  • 15% discounts on local business services and chamber events
  • 10% discount on advertisement
  • 10% discount on notary services
  • Access to seminars held by successful business owners in the area

Silver Membership - $1,000/year:

Includes Bronze Membership benefits plus these:

  • 25% discounts on local business services and chamber events
  • 25% discount on advertisement
  • 50% discount on notary services
  • 50% discount on attendance to seminars held by successful business owners in the area

Gold Membership - $2,000/year:

Includes Silver Membership benefits plus these:

  • 50% discounts on local business services and chamber events
  • 50% discount on chamber advertisement
  • FREE notary services
  • Attendance to chamber committee meetings to decide on upcoming events and updates in the area
  • FREE attendance to seminars held by successful business owners in the area
