Temple Inn & Suites - Site Plan

Purpose

Our purpose is to provide a quality and comforting place for temple patrons of all ages. We provide easy access to the temple, convenient changes rooms for travels close and far along with long term stays for those serving. We also conveniently provide short term daycare to provide ease of mind while you serve the Lord.

Audience

We want temple goers of all ages to enjoy the amenities we provide. Whether you are a new temple attendee or a family of many temple goers, we have rooms and options just for you.
